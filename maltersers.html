MALTERSERS

PREP TIME: 6 mins

YIELD: 7

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Chop savoiardi. Divide half the biscuit among four 1 1/2 cup glasses.
  2. Drizzle over half the coffee mixture. Top each with 8 Maltesers. Divide half the mascarpone mixture among glasses.
  3. Dust with cocoa powder. Top with the remaining biscuit, coffee mixture and mascarpone mixture. Top with extra Maltesers. Chill for 2-3 hours. Dust with extra cocoa powder.

CHOCOLATE BAUBLES

PREP TIME: 15 mins

YIELD: 8

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Place the chocolate cake in the bowl of a food processor and process until fine crumbs form.
  2. Combine the butter, condensed milk and brown sugar in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook, stirring, for 10 minutes or until the butter melts and the mixture thickens and comes away from the side of the pan. Remove from heat. Set aside for 30 minutes to cool slightly.
  3. Add the cake crumbs to the butter mixture and stir until well combined. Use your hands to roll 1 tablespoonful of the cake-crumb mixture around each marshmallow to enclose and form a ball.

BANANA SPLIT

PREP TIME: 13 mins

YIELD: 4

Ingredients





Steps

  1. To make chocolate sauce: Place chocolate and cream in a microwavesafe jug. Microwave, uncovered, on HIGH (100%) power for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring every minute with a metal spoon or until smooth. Stir in Kahlua, if desired.
  2. Place banana slices on a plate. Top with scoops of ice-cream.
  3. Drizzle with chocolate sauce. Sprinkle with nuts. Serve.
